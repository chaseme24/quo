List of ANSI colors supported by :func:`quo.echo`

* ``black (might be a gray)``
* ``red``
* ``green``
* ``yellow`` *(might be an orange)*
* ``blue``
* ``magenta``
* ``cyan``
* ``white`` *(might be light gray)*
* ``vblack``  *vibrant black*
* ``vblue``
* ``vmagenta``
* ``vwhite``
* ``vcyan``
* ``vred``
* ``vgreen``
* ``vyellow``
